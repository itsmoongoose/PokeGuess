Welcome to PokeGuess!

To Get Started:

  1. Spawn a Pokemon via the generate button.
  2. Enter the Pokemon's name in the search field.
  3. Submit your guess by clicking the submit button or pressing enter on your keyboard.

Your stats will be tracked as you play! However, refreshing the page or visiting the Pokedex page will reset all progress.
